About us 

Safir plastic factory is an enterprise that operates in plastic injection sector. 
Our factory that is based in zakho city/iraq has been operating since 2006. The history of sabuncu family in plastic business goes to, as far as 2003, with the plastic commode production in mosul. Due to political instability and safety problems in mosul at that time, the production have stopped. Within 2 years time, safir plastic factory established in zakho city with 1,000m2 production area.

Since the humble beginning in 2006, safir plastic reached today with continous development and growth from a single product to many types and sizes of different household utensils. The factory has been moved to its new premises in 2012 and continued to operate with a production area of 2,500m2.

Safir plastic has been serving to all iraqi market with the world?s top quality standards and met its costumers with the innovative products produced with the ??latest technology with the brand new injection machines and molds??.  Being the first&only factory in iraq using the iml (in-mold labeling) technology in our production of labeled plastic commodes have reinforsed our leadership in our sector.

Our target is, as we have done so far,  to follow up the latest technologies in the world and adapt them into our production and hence provide the best quality with the lowest possible prices to our costumers.
